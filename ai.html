What is AI, and how do programmes like ChatGPT and DeepSeek work?

AI is the simulation of human intelligence in machines. Programmes like ChatGPT and DeepSeek are large language models. They work by learning patterns from vast amounts of text data to understand and generate human-like text for various tasks like conversation and content creation.

Krishnamurti on Artificial Intelligence

Krishnamurti viewed AI as a reflection of our own programmed, mechanical thinking. He questioned humanity's essence if machines surpass our cognitive abilities, urging a shift from outward technology to inward self-awareness and a non-mechanical intelligence rooted in compassion.
